Trans* Social Support Group

Date: Wednesdays Time: 7:00pm - 9:00pm Zoom:https://zoom.us/j/276594389

Join us every Wednesday from 7:00PM - 9:00PM at the South Bay LGBTQ Center. As a peer led social-support group for Trans*, Gender Non-Conforming, Gender Fluid, Non-Binary, and Gender Variance people, we welcome anyone who might be or is questioning their Gender Identity. Friends and family members of Trans* people are welcome to attend. This is a place to discuss our community in a supportive and welcoming atmosphere.

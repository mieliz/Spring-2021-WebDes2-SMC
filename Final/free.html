Free 2B LGBT Teens Group

Date: Wednesdays Time: 5:00pm - 7:00pm Zoom: Contact Edwin Aceves at Eaceves@southbayCenter.org

Join us every Wednesday from 5:00PM - 7:00PM at the South Bay LGBTQ Center. As a peer led social-support group, we are focused on exploring sexual and gender identity in a welcoming and supportive environment. Membership is open to teenagers 13-18 years old. Identification as LGBTQ+/Queer is not required to attend.
